Our Idea

Our goal is to present to you a friendly community where everyone is welcome to join.
In the sharing of an interest or just tha joy of a community is in focus.
An envoirement that is safe and caring and a place where you're allowed to share your thoughts and emotions,
where you can meet new people and share experiences.

Our Story

We started this bookclub in 2021, in the middle of the pandamic of Covid-19,
as we noticed our mental healtch was suffering because of the lack of social interactions.
First we started with our onlinegroups, but since the restrictions was called off, we finally had our first meeting in person
and the outcome was amazing!
Since then our community has grown bigger and bigger and today we're running weekly meetings with all different groups.
